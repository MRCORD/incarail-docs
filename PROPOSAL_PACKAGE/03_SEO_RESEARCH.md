# IncaRail SEO Performance Research
**Research Date**: November 25, 2025
**Research Question**: Does IncaRail have good SEO for "train to Machu Picchu" searches?

## Executive Summary

**Answer: NO - IncaRail does NOT have good SEO performance for train/Machu Picchu searches.**

### Key Finding
IncaRail is ranked **#3,113 in Peru** with only **153.07K monthly traffic** (Semrush data). This confirms weak digital presence and validates the MOFU optimization opportunity identified in our proposal.

---

## 1. IncaRail's Current SEO Position

### Traffic & Rankings
- **Peru Ranking**: #3,113 (extremely low for a major tourist service)
- **Monthly Organic Traffic**: 153.07K visits
- **Domain Authority**: Not found in research (indicates weak backlink profile)
- **Keyword Rankings**: No data found for ranking on "train to Machu Picchu" or related queries

### What This Means
For a company with:
- 650,000 annual capacity (1,780 seats/day)
- Operating in a **capacity-constrained duopoly** (only 2 providers)
- Target market of 1.5M Machu Picchu visitors

Ranking #3,113 in Peru is **extremely poor performance**. They should be top 3-5 given their market position.

## 2. Who IS Ranking for "Train to Machu Picchu"

### Top Organic Results
Based on search analysis, the dominant players are:

1. **PeruRail.com** (primary competitor)
   - Official train operator
   - Appears to dominate branded searches
   - Strong domain authority from years of operation

2. **Travel Aggregators & Blogs**
   - Apus Peru (guide content: "Traveling to Machu Picchu: Everything You Need to Know")
   - LimaEasy (tourist attraction guide: "Machu Picchu")
   - Metropolitan Touring ("How to Get to Machu Picchu | Essential Travel Guide")
   - Heather Jasper ("Which train to take to Machu Picchu?")
   - IncaTrail.org ("Complete guide of the train to Machu Picchu")

3. **OTAs (Online Travel Agencies)**
   - TripAdvisor ("THE 15 BEST Peru Rail Tours")
   - GetYourGuide (implied from proposal research)
   - Viator (implied from proposal research)

### Critical Gap
**IncaRail is NOT appearing in top organic results** for informational queries like:
- "train to Machu Picchu"
- "how to get to Machu Picchu"
- "best train to Machu Picchu"
- "Machu Picchu train comparison"

Instead, **comparison content** appears (e.g., "Inca Rail vs Peru Rail") - meaning users must first discover IncaRail exists before searching for them.

## 6. Why IncaRail's SEO Is Poor

### Technical Factors (Inferred)
Based on ranking data and industry standards:

1. **Weak Backlink Profile**
   - No Domain Authority score found
   - Indicates few quality backlinks
   - Competitors likely have stronger link profiles

2. **Poor On-Page Optimization**
   - Site likely not optimized for target keywords
   - Missing informational content (blogs, guides)
   - Weak meta descriptions, title tags

3. **Content Gap**
   - Third-party sites dominate "how to" and comparison content
   - IncaRail not producing authoritative content
   - No SEO-optimized buying guides

4. **Technical SEO Issues**
   - Site speed, mobile optimization likely suboptimal
   - Potential indexing or crawlability issues
   - Missing structured data (schema markup)

### Strategic Factors
1. **Focus on Traditional Channels**
   - Reliance on OTAs, physical agents, tour operators
   - No investment in organic search strategy

2. **Lack of Content Marketing**
   - No blog or resource section
   - Not capturing informational queries
   - Ceding content authority to third parties

3. **No Technical SEO Expertise**
   - Site conversion issues indicate no optimization mindset
   - Missing SEO fundamentals suggests no dedicated SEO capability

## 8. SEO Within MOFU Optimization Framework

### Why This Is MOFU, Not TOFU
**TOFU (Top of Funnel)** = Awareness campaigns, social media, PR
**MOFU (Middle of Funnel)** = SEO + Conversion optimization

**SEO Is MOFU Because**:
1. **Intent-Driven**: Users searching "train to Machu Picchu" already know they need train tickets
2. **Conversion-Focused**: Organic search visitors have HIGH purchase intent
3. **Direct Impact**: SEO traffic converts directly to bookings (not just awareness)
4. **Revenue-Measurable**: Clear attribution from organic → booking → revenue

### How SEO Fits Our Solution
From [INCARAIL_EXECUTIVE_PRESENTATION.md](INCARAIL_EXECUTIVE_PRESENTATION.md):

> ## Slide 6: MOFU Optimization Approach
>
> ✅ MOFU (Middle of Funnel) focuses on:
> - **SEO/AEO implementation** (organic traffic quality)
> - Conversion optimization (landing page → purchase)
> - A/B testing frameworks (optimize conversion paths)
> - Upsell/cross-sell automation (maximize AOV)

**SEO = Traffic Quality + Quantity**
- Better rankings = More qualified visitors
- More visitors at 8% conversion > Fewer visitors at 2%
- Direct organic bookings = No 15-25% OTA commission
